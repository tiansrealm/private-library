# Take Initiative, But Don't Grovel

**Source:** Jun Ge (君哥)
**Date Added:** 2026-03-11

---

## 1. Be Proactive, Not Desperate
- Take initiative, especially early — women are often more passive
- Being proactive shows your attitude and guides the relationship, but you remain equals
- Adjust effort based on her response — more if she's responsive, less if she isn't

## 2. Prize Mindset
- Don't treat her as the prize or put her on a pedestal — **see yourself as the prize**
- Be kind and sincere, but don't let her walk over you
- Effort should reflect value, not desperation

## 3. Control Neediness
- Avoid constant messaging, instant replies, or trying to meet every day
- Excessive neediness makes you look boring, unreliable, or like you have no life

## 4. Guide Her to Invest
- A girl who likes you will contribute — materially, emotionally, or with time/effort
- Don't give endlessly; guide her to do things for you to create mutual investment
- Examples: ask her to call you to wake up, help pick clothes, show her cooking skills
- This balances the relationship and prevents her from holding all the power

## 5. Stay Relaxed and Self-Respecting
- Stay calm regardless of her responses — don't let her reactions control you
- If she responds well, engage more; if poorly, hold back
- Don't bombard with messages or grovel — maintain self-respect

---

## Core Message

> Take initiative, show value, maintain dignity, avoid neediness, encourage mutual investment, and stay emotionally independent. Attraction comes from balance, confidence, and self-respect — not desperation or one-sided effort.
