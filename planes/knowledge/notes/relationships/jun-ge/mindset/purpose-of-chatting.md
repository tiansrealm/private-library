# Purpose of Chatting

**Source:** Jun Ge (君哥)
**Date Added:** 2026-03-11

---

## Core Principle

Chatting is a means, not an end. The main purpose is to arrange face-to-face dates — not to live in a virtual relationship.

- Online flirting, messaging, or video interactions alone cannot lead to real relationships, intimacy, or marriage

---

## Key Points

### 1. Avoid Over-Investing and Losing Self-Respect
- Many men spend excessive time, energy, emotion, and money on a girl while groveling or doing things they dislike
- Treating a girl as your entire world while she invests nothing creates an unbalanced and unstable dynamic
- This leads to fear, anxiety, and a lack of control over the relationship

### 2. Mutual Investment Is Essential
- If a girl doesn't invest, she won't value or care for you
- To build a lasting relationship, make her contribute — emotionally, materially, or with time and effort
- Mutual investment creates respect, attachment, and stability

### 3. Maintain Dignity and Principles
- Never grovel, beg, or compromise yourself to please a woman
- Being too "cheap" or over-giving reduces your value in her eyes and decreases her investment

---

## Core Message

> A successful relationship requires balance: chat to arrange real-life dates, invest wisely, maintain self-respect, and ensure the woman invests in you too. Don't be a one-sided giver; become a man who commands respect and mutual commitment.
