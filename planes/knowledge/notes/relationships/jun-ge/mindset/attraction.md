# Attraction

**Source:** Jun Ge (君哥)
**Date Added:** 2026-03-11

---

## 5 Key Principles

### 1. Don't Over-Give or Grovel
- Many men believe they can win a woman by pleasing her, spending money, or doing everything she wants
- This makes you appear cheap, desperate, and easy to take for granted
- Even if you "succeed," your position in the relationship will be weak and subordinate

### 2. Relationships Require Mutual Investment
- For a relationship to be stable and lasting, both sides must contribute — emotionally, materially, and with time
- If she invests, she will care about the relationship, respect you, and be less likely to leave
- If you give everything while she gives nothing, the balance of power is lost

### 3. Attraction Comes From Self-Improvement, Not Tricks
- Don't rely on gifts, compliments, or tricks to impress her — anyone can do that
- True attraction comes from being a high-value person: emotionally intelligent, physically fit, well-groomed, funny, confident, and understanding of women
- Think of yourself as a magnet — she should be drawn to you, not the other way around

### 4. Women Value Strength and Status
- Women naturally choose strong, high-value men who enhance their image, not weak men who chase them
- Investing in yourself makes you desirable and respected, creating long-term balanced love

### 5. Keep Perspective
- Before intimacy, a girl is just someone you know — there's no inherent obligation for her to reciprocate your care
- Chasing or over-investing based on misguided rules can negatively impact your mindset for life

---

## Core Message

> Stop pleasing and groveling. Build your value, create mutual investment, and attract women naturally. Only by being a high-value man can you earn respect, love, and a balanced, lasting relationship.
