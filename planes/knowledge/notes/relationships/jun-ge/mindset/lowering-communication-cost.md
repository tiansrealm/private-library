# Lowering Communication Cost (降低沟通成本)

**Source:** Jun Ge — Alumni group case study (Sean + model girl), 2026-03-15
**Date Added:** 2026-03-17

---

## Concept

素人改造的意义就是降低沟通成本。聊天聊到点子上，妹子就会配合。

The purpose of the personal makeover is to lower communication cost. When you chat on-point, the girl cooperates.

- "聊100天的妹子，我只需要用两三天聊到暧昧情感话题" — what takes others 100 days, Jun Ge's students reach in 2-3 days
- High-value people (models, busy professionals) don't have time for idle chat. They respond to efficient, on-point communication
- If you can't get there fast, the problem isn't your chat technique — it's your fundamentals (image, value, attraction)

## Why most people fail

- 大部分人是被女生筛选的阶段，不足够让女生重视我们
- Most guys are still in the "being filtered" stage — not enough for the girl to take them seriously
- They chat about random third-party topics when the girl is already giving green lights
- 人家那么优质的人，凭什么回复你？ — why would a high-quality person bother replying to you?

## What to do instead

- If chatting keeps going but you can't escalate: 抛开聊天问题，直击本质 — set aside the chat problem, hit the essence directly (work on your value/presentation)
- Chat technique is 术 (tactics); self-value is 道 (the way)

## Closing quote

> 一个人的时间不值钱，那么你遇到的妹子时间也是不值钱。
> If your time isn't valuable, the girls you meet won't be valuable either.
