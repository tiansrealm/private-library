# Mindset: Common Mistakes & Gender Dynamics

**Source:** Jun Ge (君哥)
**Date Added:** 2026-03-11

---

## Three Common Mindset Mistakes

### 1. 患得患失 — Worried About Gains and Losses
- Quick to apologize, agrees with everything
- Looks weak and unattractive
- Signals insecurity

### 2. 过度自信 — Overconfident
- Only cares about own views
- Over-explains things she already knows
- Misses cues about what she doesn't want to discuss
- Not listening, just broadcasting

### 3. 真命天女症 — "Destiny's Girl" Syndrome
- Obsessed with one girl, convinced she's one of a kind
- **Causes:**
  - Lack of options → shows desperate intention → meet more people, hang out where the people you want to meet are
  - Ignorance of gender interaction dynamics

---

## Key Gender Difference

| | Men | Women |
|---|---|---|
| **Focus** | Results | Process |
| **Driven by** | Reason | Emotion |

- Except for really important things, women aren't talking to you with reason — they're expressing emotion
- Most men try to achieve **emotional results through rational thinking** — this doesn't work

---

## Core Principles

- **Be a normal man** — don't overthink, don't blame yourself, don't exhaust your energy
- Emotional connection comes from emotional communication, not logical arguments
