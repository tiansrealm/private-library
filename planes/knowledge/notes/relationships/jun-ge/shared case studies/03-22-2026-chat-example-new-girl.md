# Jun Ge Chat Example — Opening & Chatting with a New Girl

**Source:** Jun Ge (君哥) — Screen-recorded video lesson, 2026-03-22
**Type:** Real chat demonstration with live commentary

---

## Context

Jun Ge demonstrates chatting with a new girl on a dating app, walking through his thought process and teaching principles in real-time.

---

## Key Techniques Demonstrated

### 1. Pre-Chat Preparation: Check Her Friend Circle (朋友圈)

Before chatting, always check her WeChat Moments/profile photos.

- **Purpose:** See if she's attractive to you → gives you motivation to invest in the conversation
- **If her Moments are locked/empty:** Use creative ways to ask her to open it:
  - Humorous: "请公主殿下打开朋友圈" (Please, Your Highness, open your Moments)
  - Direct: "我想看看你朋友圈，想去了解看看你的照片" (I want to check out your Moments and see your photos)
  - Playful: "我翻了三天结果发现你朋友圈设了权限，怎么回事？你朋友圈是不是针对我？" (I scrolled for three days and found your Moments are restricted — is your Moments targeting me specifically?)
- **Your OWN Moments matter too:** If yours is empty/stale, girls won't invest in you → they can't tell who you are → creates insecurity and low perceived value

### 2. Opening with Photo Compliments

Jun Ge opens by complimenting a girl's photo directly:
- "我觉得你照片拍的好看，很可爱" (I think your photos look great, very cute)
- **Why it works:** It's direct, confident, gives her something to respond to, and immediately sets a warm tone

### 3. Handling Shit Tests / 废物测试 (Fèiwù Cèshì)

When the girl says "你嘴巴好甜" (You're so sweet-talking / You're good at talking to girls):

**DON'T:** Deny it ("No I'm not", "I don't usually do this")
**DO:** Own it with confidence. Example replies:
- "我也觉得我嘴挺甜的，你怎么知道的，经常尝吗？" (I also think I'm sweet — how do you know? Do you taste often?)
- "我不知道我是不是很会说话，但身边的人都觉得我比较老实，不知道为什么在你面前这么甜" (I don't know if I'm sweet-talking, but people around me think I'm honest — I don't know why I'm so sweet in front of you)
- "你也会被叫做小红娘" (You'd be called a little matchmaker too)

**Principle:** Stay in your frame. Confidence > defensiveness. You're not apologizing for being attractive.

### 4. Understanding What She Really Means / 理解女生表达

When she asks "你是广州的吗？" (Are you from Guangzhou?):

- **Surface question:** Are you from Guangzhou?
- **Real intent:** She wants to know where you're from / your background
- **Bad reply:** "是" (Yes) or "不是" (No) — dead end
- **Good reply:** "不是，我老家重庆的，一直在广州发展，你呢？" (No, my hometown is Chongqing, been developing in Guangzhou. What about you?)

**Principle:** Understand the direction behind her words, then extend the topic. Don't just answer literally.

### 5. Using Regional Identity as a Hook

Example for someone from Xinjiang:
- "我新疆的，朋友！你好朋友，来新疆吃烤肉" (I'm from Xinjiang, friend! Hello friend, come to Xinjiang to eat barbecue!)

**Principle:** Turn your background into an invitation — warm, confident, gives her something to engage with.

### 6. Extending Topics, Not Killing Them

When she mentions wanting to travel to Chongqing:
- Jun Ge says: "重庆确实适合旅游，适合居住" (Chongqing is indeed great for travel, great for living)
- One sentence that validates + extends → keeps conversation flowing

### 7. Double-Line Conversation (双线聊天)

Jun Ge demonstrates keeping two conversational threads going simultaneously — referenced at the end of the lesson as his technique for maintaining engagement.

## Core Principles from This Lesson

1. **Problem-solving ability > memorized lines** — Chat isn't about scripts. It's about understanding conversational direction and solving problems in real-time.
2. **Confidence is the real blocker** — "你心里知道该怎么做，但不敢做" (You know what to do in your heart, but you don't dare to do it)
3. **Understand yourself first, then understand women** — "第一个逻辑是你要了解你自己，而不是去了解女生" (The first logic is to understand yourself, not to understand women)
4. **Direction sense (方向感)** — Know where the conversation should go. Without direction, you can't reply. Jun Ge's experience = instant direction on any message.
5. **The root cause of not knowing how to reply** — You don't understand what women are expressing + you lack experience + you don't believe in yourself.
