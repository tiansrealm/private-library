# 8 Chat Techniques to Win Her Over

**Source:** Jun Ge (君哥) — Chat Insights (聊天心得)
**Translated from:** `chat-insights-zh.docx`

---

## 1. "Familiar-Style Openers" — Break the Ice Fast

**Use:** "You're here!" / "Sorry to keep you waiting!" / "Sit here, sit here…"

Don't open with stiff lines like "Hello," "Are you there?" or "Are you busy?" — these make the conversation feel like a business meeting and she'll match that energy.

Instead, treat her like a friend you're already hosting. From the first message, you enter a casual friend scenario — one second ago you were strangers, now the vibe is relaxed.

- Use fun stickers/memes instead of the plain smiley emoji (girls dread that one)
- If you must text a smile, write: "Hi there (manual smile)"
- Don't complain she's ignoring you — your stiff opener caused it

---

## 2. "Artistic Teasing" — Self-Introduction

**Use:** "May you smile with your eyes when you hear my name." (snicker emoji)

Overly formal introductions kill the conversation. Add a slightly poetic or playful line before saying your name to leave a memorable impression.

- If your real name isn't interesting, use a fun nickname
- Tell her: "You'll have to wait until we're closer before I share my real name"
- Adding mystery and teasing to ordinary chat makes her feel it's worth continuing

---

## 3. "Arrogant Humor" — Lubricate the Relationship

**Use:** "After all, I make a living with my looks… and talent."

The formula: **brag + humor to defuse**. Great for breaking initial tension.

- When she compliments you, don't humbly deflect ("Oh, I'm average") — that's boring
- Instead, playfully exaggerate: "Nonsense — I live off my looks, okay?" (smirk)
- If she says you're handsome: "After all, I make a living with my looks… and talent"
- Guys who self-mock confidently are refreshing — don't worry about bad impressions

---

## 4. "Flirt for No Reason" — Push the Relationship Forward

**Use:** "Nothing's wrong — I just called because I wanted to hear your voice."

Straight guys only contact girls when they have a reason. But girls find this confusing too.

- While at work, call her with no agenda: "Just wanted to hear your voice"
- She'll say "Are you crazy?" — stay quiet a moment, then say you need to get back to work and hang up
- This accelerates the relationship significantly
- **Only use when you already have some familiarity** — not right after meeting

---

## 5. "Tease After a Run-In" — Playful Encounter

**Use:** "You really can't keep following me like this."

Whether the encounter is planned or accidental, skip boring lines like "Oh, you're here too?"

- Tease her instead: "You really can't keep following me like this"
- She'll feel annoyed but amused — and might actually wonder if she *was* following you
- Creates a fun, cocky dynamic

---

## 6. "Confessing with Gifts" — The Right Way

**Use:** "It's not because the gift is expensive — it's because you deserve better."

Don't blindly throw gifts at every situation. A gift is basically a confession — if your relationship is good, it's a plus; if you're already groveling, it's a minus.

- Reference from *Ode to Joy*: Wang Baichuan gives Fan Shengmei a bag and says this line
- It works because it recognizes *her* value rather than pressuring her to reciprocate
- Even expensive gifts need the right words — otherwise money spent, hand still not held

---

## 7. "Warm Words That Hit Her Soft Spot"

**Use:** "Go have fun — everything's on me… but how are you going to thank me?"

Don't enter "unconditional errand-running mode" (good morning/afternoon/night, have you eaten/slept).

- First, let her talk about her problem — figure out if she wants advice or actual help
- If she genuinely needs your help, step up without hesitation
- But add: "Go have fun — everything's on me. But… how are you going to thank me?"
- She must also invest — otherwise you become a "functional backup" for dirty work

---

## 8. When Escalation Stalls — Don't Panic

**Use:** "Don't worry — we're not having sex tonight."

When physical escalation hits resistance, don't freeze or push harder.

- Say: "We're not having sex tonight…" — this instantly calls out what she's thinking
- She'll be confused: "If not that, then what?"
- Answer: just kissing. "I just want to kiss you. Got a problem?"
- The confusion and your confident lead create half-resistance, half-compliance
- **Key principle:** Attraction is an emotional activity — if you can't create emotional waves, nothing you say matters

---

## Core Takeaway

Chatting isn't about clever tricks — it's about understanding her mindset and doing things that move her emotions. Whether you just met or are already intimate, if you can't chat well and hit the right emotional notes, the relationship won't progress. Boldly engage her emotions — that's how flirting experts are made.
