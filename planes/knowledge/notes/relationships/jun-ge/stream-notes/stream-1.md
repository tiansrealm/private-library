# Stream 1 — Chatting Examples & Intimacy Testing

**Source:** Jun Ge (君哥) — Live Stream
**Date Added:** 2026-03-11

---

## Chatting Notes

- **Test raising intimacy** (测试升温 / cèshì shēngwēn) — probe to see if she's ready for the next level
- Don't obsess over what you did wrong — move forward
- Change her topic to your topic — guide the conversation
- If a girl is asking questions about you → she's interested
- **Dual-thread chatting** increases response rate >80%
- Don't make demands
- 形象是走在能力的前面 — Image precedes ability
- Confidence is important
- Can create discussion from emoticons
- **Continue if she expresses agreement. Retreat if she resists** → restart from previous stage

---

## 6 Signs You Don't Know How to Chat

1. Scared to send/post — care too much about others' opinions
2. Self-deprecation
3. Keep thinking about the other person instead of yourself
4. Negative energy overflowing
5. Can't manage emotions
6. Listening to the nagging of people around you
