## Chat Rules

- No topic right now? **Create one** or extend a previous one
- If the relationship is close, you can talk about anything — **Relationship > Language**
- When she shares her life → express your thoughts and feelings
  - She posts cake → "Looks delicious, little foodie" (light, positive reaction)
  - **Never say anything negative** about her or her choices
- Do NOT respond to topics that are harmful to you — just ignore them. Respond to topics that benefit you
- **Chatting focuses on emotion, not the topic itself**
- In person: pair actions with words to create emotion
- Most girls like **strong, assertive men** — not obedient ones
- 爱人先爱己 — Love yourself before loving others
