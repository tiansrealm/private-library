## 3 Stages of Emotional Intelligence

### Stage 1: 到死 (Disliked / Hated by Girls)
- Don't learn, don't improve
- Stuck in the same patterns

### Stage 2: Normal Person
- No problem finding a partner, but **lacking options**
- Tend to be stubborn — don't believe they can improve
- Stops growing, gives up easily

### Stage 3: High-Quality Man
- Has options
- Has the ability to solve problems
- Continuously improving
