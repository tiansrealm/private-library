# Stream 3 — Q&A: Common Mistakes

**Source:** Jun Ge (君哥) — Live Stream
**Date Added:** 2026-03-11

---

## Q1: Why does being eager push her away?

**Problem:** Emotions become unstable and impatient. You want to "lock down" the relationship, but she keeps more distance.

**Answer:**
- Your intention is too strong — you're not providing the process she wants
- Your needs are much greater than hers → you're pressuring her
- Just copying chat scripts isn't enough — you need emotional understanding
- If you act too desperate, they retreat
- **Show value. Understand her values. Let her feel comfortable with you.**

---

## Q2: Why does rushing into a relationship make her feel less secure?

**Answer:**
- You have too few options and always put her first
- A woman will always put herself first
- **You have to put yourself first too** — not always make the girl the priority

---

## Q3: Why does flaunting money backfire?

**Problem:** Awkwardly and forcefully showing "financial" value makes her disgusted.

**Answer:**
- Because aside from financial value, you don't have anything else that stands out
- **Show more internal values** — don't lean on money alone
