# Foundation: Attraction & Self-Improvement

## 1. Walk Out From Past Mistakes

- **Law of competition** — dating is competitive; women choose, men are the product being chosen
- Any man can be "good to a woman" — that alone doesn't differentiate you
- Women choosing you should feel like they're making the right choice — they need to pay a price for choosing you
- Being good to a woman is only effective **after** she's already attracted
- It's not what you did for her — it's whether you've outperformed the competition
- **Find the problem with yourself. Make yourself attractive.**

## 2. How Is Charm Produced?

- **Don't chase. Attract.**
- Chasing = sales pitch. Nobody buys a product they think is bad just because a salesman is pushing it
- Attraction = she chooses you herself
  - She needs to think you're worth choosing
  - The base of conversation is attraction and compatibility
  - If the base isn't there, even great conversation just means she likes the conversation — not you

## 3. A Winner's Mindset

- 君子求自己，小人求他人 — "A gentleman looks within himself; a petty person blames others"
- Don't blame others. Find the problem with yourself. Only you are responsible for yourself
- Accept your imperfect self to be able to change for the better

## 4. Displaying Your Charm

### External Attraction (determines if she wants to interact)

| Factor | Key Point |
|---|---|
| **Appearance** | First impressions matter — people don't see your internal qualities until they spend time with you |
| **Career** | Let them believe you have potential |
| **Lifestyle** | Determines compatibility — what do you do outside work? Have your own plan to be different, better |
| **Taste (品味)** | Refinement in choices and preferences |

### Internal Attraction (determines how long she stays)

| Factor | Key Point |
|---|---|
| **Action** | Do the right thing at the right stage. Understand what actions she likes or dislikes |
| **Mindset** | Respect yourself. If she doesn't respond, don't overly care |
| **Thought (思想)** | Are your worldviews compatible? Show you can see things from her perspective |

### How to Display Value

1. **Self-image building**
   - Construct your character — everyone is busy, people don't have time for lengthy discovery
   - Cooking skill photos, classy locations, curated social presence

2. **Social media presence**
   - Without social media presence, it takes more time and effort
   - Can be targeted to a particular woman (e.g., if she likes animals, display animals)

3. **Dates with purpose**
   - Lead the date. Push the relationship forward
   - Test receptiveness with small gestures (e.g., clean food from her mouth)
   - Movie + send her home after (gives sense of security)
