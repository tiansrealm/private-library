# The Basic Needs of the Male and Female in Marriage

**Source:** Dr. Myles Munroe — YouTube ([wKM2SY7_2UI](https://youtube.com/watch?v=wKM2SY7_2UI))
**Date Added:** 2026-03-11

---

## Core Framework

Men and women have 5 fundamental needs each. These are **needs**, not wants — like gasoline to a car. The needs are paired opposites, reflecting how men and women are designed differently.

| # | Male Need | Female Need |
|---|---|---|
| 1 | Sexual fulfillment | Affection |
| 2 | Recreational companionship | Conversation |
| 3 | An attractive wife | Honesty and openness |
| 4 | Domestic support | Financial support |
| 5 | Admiration and respect | Family commitment |

---

## Male Needs — Detail

### 1. Sexual Fulfillment
- Men don't *want* sex, they *need* it
- Women like sex but need affection — different drivers

### 2. Recreational Companionship
- Men are designed as protectors → possessive of territory → competitive spirit
- This is why men love sports — it feeds the protector/competitor instinct
- A man wants his wife to enjoy his interests and participate in his activities
- **Practical:** Study what your husband likes and learn to do it with him

### 3. An Attractive Wife
- Men are stimulated by sight (women by sound)
- A wife should stay "chasable" — the biblical word "cling/cleave" means to chase
- You're supposed to look *better* after marriage, not let go
- Christ keeps his bride (the church) beautiful — the model for husbands too

### 4. Domestic Support
- Home should be a man's refuge and safe haven
- A clean, orderly, peaceful home — not a place of interrogation
- If home isn't safe, men escape to friends, bars, anywhere else
- He doesn't want this — he *needs* it

### 5. Admiration and Respect
- Men are designed to carry responsibility for the family
- What they need is encouragement; what they usually get is criticism
- Criticism drives men away from responsibility
- **Find something positive** — even small things — and praise it
- Why men stay in male friend groups: other men praise and affirm them
- Why boys join gangs: gangs give them the admiration they don't get at home

---

## Female Needs — Detail

### 1. Affection
- Women don't want affection, they *need* it
- Affection ≠ sex — it's the emotional connection

### 2. Conversation
- Women are stimulated by what they hear
- They need to talk *and* be listened to
- **Key insight:** When a woman brings a problem, she often doesn't want you to solve it — she wants you to listen. If you just listen, she walks away happy

### 3. Honesty and Openness
- A woman needs a man who tells her everything — plans, thoughts, decisions
- Don't make business decisions or investments without discussing with your wife first
- She may agree, but she needs to know first

### 4. Financial Support
- Even if she works, she still needs financial provision ("plenty")
- Women are designed as receivers; men as givers
- Deep down, most women don't want to *have to* work

### 5. Family Commitment
- She needs to know you are committed only to her and the children
- Women are emotional creatures who need security
- Security is the foundation — without it, the relationship crumbles

---

## Key Design Differences

| Dimension | Male | Female |
|---|---|---|
| Stimulated by | Sight | Sound |
| Response to betrayal | Feels violated (territory taken) → aggression | Feels guilty, abandoned, hurt → depression |
| Core drive | Protection / possession | Security / connection |
| Expression | Competitive, possessive | Conversational, receptive |

---

## Munroe's Closing Point

All of this requires agape love (love without conditions), which requires the Holy Spirit. The framework is practical, but the power to live it comes from spiritual transformation.
