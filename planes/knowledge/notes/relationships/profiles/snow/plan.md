# Snow / Nami — Relationship Strategy Plan

**Created:** 2026-03-13
**Framework:** 5-Stage Relationship Model (Jun Ge) + Anxious-Avoidant Recovery
**Current Stage:** 4–5 (Intimacy → Life)
**Objective:** Stabilize the relationship by breaking the anxious-avoidant cycle. Become the fun, secure partner she chooses to move toward — not the stressed, over-giving one she feels obligated to.

---

## Current Assessment

**Bridge position:** Intimacy → Life (crossing, but stalled by recurring conflict cycles)

The bond is real — mutual love, physical intimacy, family-level framing ("我已经把你当成家人了"), shared vulnerability. But the anxious-avoidant dynamic keeps pulling it backward. Every time things are good, a stress trigger (silence, busyness, mental health dip) fires the pursue-withdraw loop.

**Her image of you (current):** Loving but intense. Gives a lot but demands a lot. Associated with both warmth AND pressure.
**Her image of you (target):** Stable, fun, secure. Someone whose presence feels like relief, not obligation.

---

## The 5 Strategic Pillars

### 1. Be the Fun Option, Not the Emergency Contact

~60% of past interactions revolve around legal docs, money, medication, mental health crises, and conflict. She associates you with stress.

**Actions:**
- Lead conversations with energy, humor, interesting things — not logistics or problems
- Plan dates around *fun* (KTV, art museum, VR worked — do more of that)
- Share project wins, funny moments, random observations
- Save heavy topics for in-person, side-by-side (walking, driving — never text at 2am)

**Test:** If 3 out of your last 5 messages are fun/light, you're on track. If 3 out of 5 are about problems, course-correct.

### 2. One-Message Rule (Non-Negotiable)

The worst moments follow: she goes silent → you stack 3-5 messages → she feels cornered → blows up.

**Actions:**
- Send one message. Phone down.
- If no reply in 24h, one calm check-in. That's it.
- Never escalate silence into threats ("I'll call the police") or guilt ("你是不是不在乎我")
- Silence ≠ rejection. She processes internally. Give her room.

### 3. Let Her Come to You

You initiate most plans, emotional conversations, and repairs. She never has to reach toward you. This kills her investment.

**Actions:**
- After any meeting, don't initiate for 2-3 days
- When she initiates (like today's dream message), reward it with warm engagement
- Track initiation ratio — aim for closer to 50/50 over time
- If she doesn't reach out for a while, that's data, not a crisis

### 4. Stop Over-Giving

Over-giving creates obligation, not attraction. She feels indebted → guilty → resentful.

**Actions:**
- Don't offer help she didn't ask for
- Keep finances separate — no more Zelle for her expenses, revisit joint card
- Let her do things for you too — mutual investment is the goal
- When she says "我一个人可以搞得定", believe her

### 5. Regulate Yourself First

Your depression/anxiety amplifies every friction point. When you're dysregulated, you escalate. When you escalate, she withdraws. When she withdraws, you spiral.

**Actions:**
- Stay on medication, keep therapy
- Don't make her your sole emotional regulator — maintain projects, friendships, exercise
- When triggered: pause, breathe, wait 30 minutes before responding
- Her advice is actually right: "先照顾好自己再去爱"

## Conversation Tactics

### When She Initiates with Warmth (like today)
- Match her energy — playful, light, fun
- Use scene-building (画面感) to pull her into shared moments
- Don't escalate to heavy ("我也天天想你" kills the vibe)
- Reward her initiation with engagement — this trains the dynamic you want

### When She's Stressed / Distant
- One supportive message: "辛苦了，忙完了我们出去放松一下"
- Don't probe ("怎么了？你还好吗？你是不是生气了？") — let her come to you
- Offer presence, not solutions: "我在" > "你应该这样做..."

### When Conflict Starts
- De-escalate: "我不想吵架，我们都在乎对方，明天见面再说好不好"
- If you're triggered: put the phone down. Reply in 30 minutes minimum
- Never text-fight past midnight — nothing good comes from it

### Date Ideas (Fun-First)
- Repeat what worked: KTV, art museum, VR, movies
- New: cooking together, walk the High Line, Brooklyn Botanic Garden, board game café
- Keep dates low-logistics — don't overplan. Spontaneous > elaborate
