# Esther — Live Coach Log — 2026-03-13

## Strategy: Frame Flip — Make Her Chase

**Goal:** Play the mental game. No money invested. Flip the power dynamic — she's used to controlling the frame as gatekeeper/seller. Break that pattern.

### Tactics
1. **Never discuss money or scheduling** — deflect with humor if she steers transactional
2. **Push-pull** — warmth then withdraw. Be fun but slightly out of reach
3. **Make her invest** — ask things requiring effort (selfies, recommendations, real talk)
4. **Tease the flattery** — call out scripted compliments playfully ("你这话跟每个人都说吧😏")
5. **One-message rule** — never stack. Send one, wait.

### Prepared Lines
- "你这话跟每个人都说吧😏" / "哈哈 你这句台词练了多少遍" — flattery deflectors
- "你最近在干嘛 还在纽约吗" — casual re-engage
- "哈哈 不笑你 就是突然想起来你了" — warm pull
- "发个你今天的自拍看看" / "给我推荐个好吃的店" — investment requests
