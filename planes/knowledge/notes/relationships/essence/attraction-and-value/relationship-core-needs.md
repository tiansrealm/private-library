# Male & Female Core Needs

**Sources:** Dr. Myles Munroe ([basic-needs-male-female](../../dr-myles-monroe/basic-needs-male-female.md))
**Last Updated:** 2026-03-11

---

## The 5 Core Needs

These are **needs**, not preferences. Unmet needs cause the relationship to break down — like a car without fuel.

### What He Needs

| Need | What It Means | How to Meet It |
|---|---|---|
| **Sexual fulfillment** | Physical intimacy is a core need, not optional | Prioritize physical connection; don't treat it as a reward |
| **Recreational companionship** | He wants you to enjoy his world with him | Learn his interests, participate, share the experience |
| **Attractive partner** | He's visual — appearance matters throughout the relationship | Stay "chasable" — invest in yourself after marriage, not just before |
| **Domestic support** | Home = his safe haven, not a warzone | Create a peaceful, orderly home environment |
| **Admiration & respect** | Encouragement fuels responsibility; criticism kills it | Find positives and praise them — even small things |

### What She Needs

| Need | What It Means | How to Meet It |
|---|---|---|
| **Affection** | Emotional warmth and physical tenderness (not just sex) | Show consistent affection — touch, words, presence |
| **Conversation** | She needs to talk and be heard | Listen without solving. Her talking *is* the solution |
| **Honesty & openness** | Full transparency on plans, thoughts, decisions | Share everything proactively — don't make her ask |
| **Financial support** | Security through provision, even if she works | Provide abundantly; don't assume her income covers her need |
| **Family commitment** | Assurance that you're committed to her and the children only | Demonstrate loyalty consistently — she needs to feel secure |

---

## Key Principles

1. **Needs are non-negotiable** — unmet needs don't go away, they drive people toward whoever meets them
2. **Men and women are wired differently** — he's visual, she's auditory; he protects territory, she seeks security
3. **Criticism destroys men; silence destroys women** — praise him, talk to her
4. **Listen, don't fix** — when she shares a problem, she usually wants a listener, not a solution
5. **Stay invested post-commitment** — the relationship requires *more* effort after marriage, not less
