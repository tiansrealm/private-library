## The Three Mindset Traps

### 1. 患得患失 — Anxious About Gains and Losses
- Quick to apologize, agrees with everything, scared to act
- Signals insecurity — looks weak and unattractive

### 2. 过度自信 — Overconfident / Not Listening
- Only cares about own views, over-explains, misses cues
- Broadcasting, not communicating — kills connection

### 3. 真命天女症 — "Destiny's Girl" Syndrome
- Obsessed with one girl, convinced she's the only one
- Caused by lack of options → shows desperate intention
- Fix: meet more people, expand your social world

## Three Dimensions of EQ

### 1. Perceive Others' Emotions
- **Read faces** — 70% of emotion shows on the face. If she looks uncomfortable, you said something wrong
- **Read responses** — short replies ("Nice," "Good") = she's ending the topic. Don't monologue.
- **Sense atmosphere** — "I'm not upset" often means accumulated frustrations you missed

### 2. Control Your Own Emotions
- Maturity stages: Explosion → Suppression → Calm detachment
- You know which words hurt most — EQ means not saying them
- Replace "No, that's wrong" with "I see your point, but…"
- If she cries over something small, comfort — don't prove logic

### 3. Align Behavior with Goals
- **Women care about attitude more than actions** — "I have to wake up early, but I've missed talking to you" → she'll let you rest
- If she wants apples and you give bananas, your effort doesn't matter
- "Being nice" ≠ "she will date me" — that's behavior contradicting your goal

## Initiative Without Groveling

1. **Be proactive, not desperate** — take initiative (especially early), but adjust based on her response
2. **Prize mindset** — see yourself as the prize, not her. Be kind but don't let her walk over you
3. **Control neediness** — no constant messaging, instant replies, or trying to meet daily
4. **Guide her to invest** — ask her to do things for you (call to wake up, help pick clothes). Mutual investment prevents her from holding all the power
5. **Stay relaxed** — don't let her reactions control your emotions. Responsive? Engage more. Cold? Hold back.

## Why Eagerness Pushes Her Away

- Your intention is too strong — you're not providing the process she wants
- Your needs are much greater than hers → you're pressuring her
- Copying chat scripts isn't enough — you need emotional understanding
- Having too few options makes you put her first; **you have to put yourself first too**
- 爱人先爱己 — Love yourself before loving others

## Purpose of Chatting

Chatting is a **means**, not an end. The purpose is to arrange face-to-face dates — not to live in a virtual relationship.

- Don't treat a girl as your entire world while she invests nothing
- Never grovel, beg, or compromise yourself to please a woman
- Maintain dignity and principles — being too "cheap" reduces your value in her eyes
