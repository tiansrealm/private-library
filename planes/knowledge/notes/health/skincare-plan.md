## Skin Pattern Observations

### Nose
Issues:
- Large pores
- Frequent blackheads
- Chronic redness
- Visible small blood vessels

Likely causes:
- High oil production in T-zone
- Possible mild vascular rosacea
- Heat-triggered blood vessel dilation

Patterns observed:
- Worse in hot summer
- Better in winter
- Flares together with acne inflammation

Treatment focus:
- Salicylic acid to clear pores
- Retinoid to normalize pore turnover
- Niacinamide for redness control
- Optional metronidazole for persistent redness

---

### Forehead
Issues:
- Deep inflamed acne

Likely causes:
- Stress
- Poor sleep
- Sweat and hairline oil
- Incomplete cleansing

Treatment focus:
- Retinoid
- Benzoyl peroxide
- Thorough cleansing near hairline

---

### Nose / Mouth Area
Issues:
- Whiteheads

Possible causes:
- High glycemic / sugar diet
- Oil accumulation

Treatment focus:
- Salicylic acid
- Retinoid
- Diet moderation
